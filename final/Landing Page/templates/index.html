A website that uses advanced algorithms and fact-checking techniques to detect and flag false or misleading news stories and articles. These websites aim to counter the spread of misinformation and provide accurate information to the public.

Feature

Clipboard Verify

This feature that ensures that the text or information copied to the clipboard is authentic and accurate. It uses various techniques, such as source verification and fact-checking, to confirm the veracity of the information before it is copied to the clipboard.

Read Verify

Read verified is a feature that guarantees the accuracy and authenticity of the information presented to the user. It uses various techniques such as source verification, fact-checking, and content validation to ensure that the information is reliable and trustworthy.

Scan Verify

Scan verified is a feature that allows users to quickly and easily verify the authenticity of a document or item by scanning it using their device's camera. This feature uses advanced technologies, such as image recognition and optical character recognition (OCR), to compare the scanned item with verified sources and databases to ensure that it is legitimate and not counterfeit or altered in any way.
